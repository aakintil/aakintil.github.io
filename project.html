This was my Communication Design Fundamentals final project. Our assignment was to create a booklet about anything we want. I decided to create a couple spreads about different kinds teas. Each tea originated from a different region of the world and some had special back stories. Some of the teas were borrowed from my roommate, while the others were given to me courtesy of the owners of Margaret's Fine Imports
